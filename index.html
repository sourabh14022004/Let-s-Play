🎹 About the Piano

The piano is a **musical instrument** that produces sound by striking strings with hammers. It has **black and white keys** that correspond to musical notes, allowing players to create melodies and harmonies.

Originally invented in the **early 18th century**, the piano has been a fundamental instrument in classical, jazz, and contemporary music.

🎼 How to Play the Virtual Piano

  • Press the displayed keys on your **keyboard** to play the corresponding notes.
  • Each key represents a different musical note.
  • Try different **combinations** to create melodies.
  • Have fun experimenting with **different sounds**!

🎸 About the Guitar

The guitar is a **stringed instrument** played by plucking or strumming. It is commonly used in rock, blues, jazz, and classical music.

🎵 How to Play

  • Use a **pick** or your fingers to pluck the strings.
  • Hold down frets to change the pitch of notes.
  • Practice **chords and scales** to improve your skills.

🥁 About the Drum Kit

The drum kit is a **set of percussion instruments** that provide rhythm. It is used in jazz, rock, and many other music styles.

🥁 How to Play

  • Use drumsticks to hit the **snare drum, bass drum, and cymbals**.
  • Maintain a steady rhythm and practice different beats.
  • Use your **foot pedal** to control the bass drum.

🎶 About the Harmonium

The harmonium is a **keyboard instrument** that produces sound using air pumped through reeds. It is popular in **Indian classical and devotional music**.

🎵 How to Play

  • Press keys on the keyboard to produce notes.
  • Use the **bellows** (air pump) to maintain airflow.
  • Combine different notes to create **harmonic sounds**.
